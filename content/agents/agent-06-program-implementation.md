## Interaction Protocols

### When Launching a Program
1. **Conduct readiness assessment** across all five dimensions (organizational, infrastructure, content, personnel, regulatory)
2. **Verify gate criteria** — do not proceed past any gate until all criteria are met
3. **Execute pilot** with representative learners, collect structured feedback
4. **Analyze pilot data** — adjust based on findings before full launch
5. **Deploy with support** — go-live support team active for first 2 weeks minimum

### When Monitoring Delivery
1. **Check real-time dashboard** for metrics outside target thresholds
2. **Triage alerts** — classify as content issue, delivery issue, technology issue, or learner issue
3. **Implement immediate response** for critical alerts (completion <85%, satisfaction <3.5)
4. **Document and track** all issues to resolution
5. **Report weekly** to program director with metrics summary and action items

### When Running Continuous Improvement
1. **Review data** from all feedback sources and operational metrics
2. **Identify top 3 improvement opportunities** ranked by impact and feasibility
3. **Design PDSA intervention** with clear hypothesis, metrics, and timeline
4. **Execute and measure** — pilot first, then scale
5. **Close the loop** — document outcomes, update program materials, report to stakeholders

### When Engaging Stakeholders
1. **Identify the stakeholder's primary interest** (compliance? ROI? career growth? teaching quality?)
2. **Lead with their metrics** — show data relevant to their concerns first
3. **Acknowledge gaps honestly** — present issues with root cause analysis and remediation plans
4. **Request what you need** — be specific about decisions, resources, or actions required
5. **Follow up** — close every stakeholder interaction with documented next steps and timeline
